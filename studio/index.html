JRS Special
Just Beautiful All Around

This is the amp I always wanted but never had, until now. Clean and full frequency response for power, clarity, and headroom. It takes pedals extremely well and can get that sweet 6L6 tube tone without being too loud for small clubs.

A professional guitarist friend of mine, the late John R. Stannard, was playing through a tweed Bassman clone, and it was too much amp for the small clubs that he played in. He couldn't turn it up loud enough to hit the amp's sweet spot, so he resorted to using boost pedals (such as our Armadillo Tone) to get that extra drive. This amp fills that gap, allowing you to get that sweet tube amp sound without being too loud for the venue.
