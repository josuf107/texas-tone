The Heartbreak Deluxe
Ted Nugent "Stranglehold" Tone and Dynamic Reverb!

This custom tube amp has a super clean Fender-style preamp, coupled with a power amp especially designed for early breakup, for that wonderful, creamy power tube distortion.

The result is a dynamo that can play cleans until driven hard, and then it opens up to a Nugent-style monster. 14-Watts of dynamite, with a spring reverb that is dynamic and touch sensitive, and has separate volume and tone controls. Incredible!
