Cables

Texas Tone® guitar cables are proudly made by hand in Austin, Texas, the same as all Texas Tone® Amp products using only high quality components:

  • Switchcraft® 1/4" plugs
  • Amphenol Pro 1/4" plugs
  • Mogami® W2524 shielded cable
  • Techflex 1/4" Flexo PET (polyethylene terepthalate) expandable sleeving

Mogami W2524 cable was designed for excellent sound and durablility. Its high purity copper shielding ensures very low noise, while the high purity copper center conductor conveys ultimate tone. The cross-linked polyethylene dielectric is heat resistant and will not shrink as quickly as PVC. The cable is also designed to prevent microphonics with a conductive carbon impregnated polymer sub-shield placed under the shield conductor. If you want the best cable to go from your instrument to your Texas Tone amp (or any other rig), then this is it.

Available with Techflex 1/4" Flexo PET (polyethylene terepthalate) expandable sleeving, resistant to chemical degradation, UV radiation, and abrasion.

Stocked in 12' and 15' lengths with one straight and/or right-angle USA-made Switchcraft or Amphenol Pro Range plugs. Other lengths and configurations available upon request. Lifetime limited warranty.
